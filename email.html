Hello Dodgeballers,

We will be hosting some open plays on Mondays and Thursdays in December at Hartley House located 413 W 46th Street. Our first open play is this upcoming Monday.

There is one court only, so sign up is required to attend. For the first 20 sign ups in each session will receive a confirmation email stating they are confirmed to attend the open play. Please sign up below.

The open play session is as followed:

1) Session 1 (7:00pm-8:30pm) - Big Ball Dodgeball

2) Session 2 (8:30pm-10:00pm) - Small Ball Dodgeball

Emails for sign ups for Monday open plays will go out on Fridays at 6pm and emails for sign ups for Thursday open plays will go out on Mondays at 6pm.

Please feel free to email us at dodgeball@bigapplerecsports.com if you have any questions.

Thank you,

The Big Apple Dodgeball Leadership Team
